# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive behavior:** empathy, respectful disagreement, constructive feedback, accountability.

**Unacceptable behavior:** harassment, trolling, personal attacks, publishing private information
without permission, or other unprofessional conduct.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove contributions that violate this code. Report issues via
[GitHub](https://github.com/akhilvydyula).
